Our history

Now that's a really long story there! Lets go in a nutshell

In 2016, a group of Engineering experts thought that a seperate organization was needed to govern the internet. Note that these people were closely linked to the law and FBI. Soon, they sent their plans to the FBI, along with Google and Microsoft after a year of planning, They mapped out everything and got the resource funds aswell. Soon till late 2018, everything was ready. In 2019 they launched the UDDS with the help of Google and Microsoft as a covert agency which will monitor the users of the internet but NEVER gain popularity

But staying hidden wasn't that easy. Therfore, we built our site on a less famous website, Netlify. Thanks to theri cooperation and our team's coding experts, We have managed to make this site what it is today
