How do we work?

  • Searching the internet

    We have hundred's or thousands of people which search the internet daily for any unusual activity. We partner with companies like Google or Microsoft and toghether, help find suspicious sites, users and even text messeges or activity.

  • Noting an offense

    Once we have found out the offense, we deal with it dependning on the type of offense and it's severety. Severe offenses can also land you in your countries prison if we send the offense to your goverment. But don't worry, that's rare and only for ultra severe offenses

  • Helping the offender redeem himself/herself

    We usually send the offender an E - Mail with instructions on how to stop these emails and the surviellence which is placed on them.This usually leads them to this website and the UDSS email page. They follow the instructions and boom! It's done!
