How do we keep the internet safe?

Now this is a pretty long story again! Go on reading!

In a nutshell

We have a taskforce of hundreds and thousands of men who respond to any calls made by Google, Microsoft or any other internet security manager.

A fact!

Our TaskForce usually works 8 hours a day! But they mostly stay at home so this is acceptable

Our teams

One of our team divisions, which focuses on watching over the internet, constantly keeps switching between servers and networks to find out illegal websites and purge them and their users. Another team, responds to emergency calls usually done by the FBI, Google and Microsoft. This is a bit too over simplified but this is all you need to know. Just keep in mind things are a lot more complex in real life. We have just given an over simplification

Steps our teams take to purge websites

Our team takes a variety of steps to make sure no traces of the website are left. Note,: we first take care of the website and the users being fully active on it at the same time. We have team branches for each of these specified tasks. Anyways, here's how we do it:

  • We first locate the website using our brilliant means.
  • We locate it's physichal servers and leave the arresting to the FBI while we use DDOS attacks and a variety of offensive attacks to bring the site down.
  • Befoe bringing it completely down, we track and log the users who were active on the site.
  • We log every detail of the site and also at the same time, track down the users
  • Note we do all of this anoyomously EXCEPT the user tracking part.
